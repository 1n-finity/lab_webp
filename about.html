About Me

I am Dr. P. Muthu Austeria, Assistant Professor in the Department of Chemistry at Jain University, Bangalore. My research interests lie in the field of computational chemistry, focusing on understanding molecular properties and chemical processes through theoretical and computational approaches.

I completed my Ph.D. in Computational Chemistry from Pondicherry University in 2016, followed by extensive postdoctoral research experience at prestigious institutions including the Indian Institute of Science (Bangalore), Jeonbuk National University (South Korea), and KENTECH (South Korea).

My research journey began as a Junior Research Fellow at Pondicherry University in 2008, where I developed a strong foundation in computational methods and theoretical chemistry. Over the years, I have gained extensive international research experience, particularly in South Korea, where I worked on cutting-edge computational chemistry projects.

Research Philosophy

My approach to computational chemistry is centered on bridging the gap between theoretical understanding and practical applications. I believe that computational methods are powerful tools that can provide deep insights into molecular behavior and guide experimental research.

Through my work, I aim to contribute to the fundamental understanding of chemical processes and develop computational strategies that can be applied to real-world problems in chemistry, materials science, and related fields.
