Professional Experience

My professional journey in computational chemistry spans over 15 years, focusing on catalysis discovery and materials design. From Junior Research Fellow to current position, I have developed expertise at prestigious institutions in India and South Korea, specializing in DFT-based approaches to catalyst design, energy materials, and gas sensing applications.

Academic Leadership

Currently serving as Assistant Professor at Jain University, leading the computational catalysis research group. Developing DFT-based research programs, supervising students in catalyst design and energy materials, and establishing collaborations in gas sensing and battery applications.

International Research

Extensive postdoctoral experience in South Korea at KENTECH and Jeonbuk National University (2021-2023). Specialized in energy-related computational catalysis, DFT calculations for materials design, and international research collaboration in battery and sensing applications.

Premier Institution Research

Five years of postdoctoral and research associate experience at the Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore (2015-2021). Developed expertise in advanced computational methods, independent research project development, and collaboration with leading researchers.

Graduate Research Excellence

PhD research experience at Pondicherry University (2008-2015) progressing from Junior to Senior Research Fellow. Established foundation in computational chemistry, advanced method training, and conference presentations.

Teaching & Mentoring

Comprehensive experience in mentoring junior researchers, supervising graduate and undergraduate research projects, developing computational chemistry curriculum, and fostering the next generation of computational chemists.

Research Impact

Over 15 years of continuous research contribution through publications, conference presentations, international collaborations, and method development. Expertise spanning quantum chemistry, molecular dynamics, and energy applications.
