Publication Philosophy

My approach to scientific publication emphasizes rigorous computational methodology, reproducible results, and clear communication of theoretical insights. I strive to contribute meaningful advances to the field of computational chemistry through:

  • Comprehensive theoretical analysis and validation
  • Clear presentation of computational methods and parameters
  • Discussion of practical implications and applications
  • Collaboration with experimental and theoretical researchers
  • Open science practices and data sharing where appropriate

Research Collaboration

I am always interested in collaborative research projects that can advance our understanding of chemical systems through computational approaches. If you are working on related research or have questions about computational chemistry methods, please feel free to reach out.

Open to Collaboration In:

  • Joint theoretical and experimental studies
  • Method development and benchmarking
  • Interdisciplinary applications of computational chemistry
  • Student research projects and mentoring
