Research Interests

Quantum Chemistry

Advanced quantum chemical calculations to understand molecular electronic structure and properties. Utilizing density functional theory (DFT) and ab initio methods to investigate chemical bonding, molecular orbitals, and electronic transitions.

Molecular Dynamics

Computational simulations of molecular systems and their dynamic behavior. Studying molecular motion, conformational changes, and thermodynamic properties using classical and quantum mechanical approaches.

Chemical Processes

Theoretical investigation of chemical reactions and catalytic processes. Exploring reaction mechanisms, transition states, and energy barriers to understand and predict chemical reactivity.

Photonics & Biophotonics

Computational studies of light-matter interactions in biological and chemical systems. Investigating optical properties, photochemical processes, and applications in photonic devices.

Current Research Focus

Computational Modeling of Molecular Systems

Developing and applying computational methods to understand the behavior of complex molecular systems. This includes studying molecular conformations, intermolecular interactions, and the effects of environmental factors on molecular properties.

Electronic Structure Calculations

Investigating the electronic structure of molecules using advanced quantum chemical methods. Focus on understanding how electronic properties relate to molecular function and reactivity.

Photochemical Process Modeling

Computational study of photochemical reactions and light-induced processes in molecules. Understanding how light absorption leads to chemical transformations and developing models for photonic applications.

Computational Methods & Techniques

Quantum Mechanical Methods

  • Density Functional Theory (DFT)
  • Hartree-Fock (HF) calculations
  • Post-HF methods (MP2, CCSD)
  • Time-dependent DFT (TD-DFT)

Molecular Simulation

  • Classical molecular dynamics
  • Monte Carlo simulations
  • Enhanced sampling techniques
  • Free energy calculations

Specialized Techniques

  • Reaction coordinate mapping
  • Transition state optimization
  • Potential energy surface scanning
  • Statistical thermodynamics

Research Impact & Applications

Drug Discovery

Computational methods assist in understanding molecular interactions between drugs and biological targets.

Materials Science

Predicting properties of new materials and optimizing molecular structures for specific applications.

Environmental Chemistry

Understanding chemical processes in environmental systems and developing green chemistry solutions.

Energy Applications

Designing molecules and materials for energy storage, conversion, and photovoltaic applications.
