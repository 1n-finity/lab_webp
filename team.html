Our Research Team

Our computational chemistry research group is a dynamic team of researchers at various stages of their academic careers. We foster a collaborative environment where team members work together to advance our understanding of molecular systems through computational approaches.

Principal Investigator

Dr. P. Muthu Austeria

Assistant Professor & Group Leader

Department of Chemistry, Jain University

Ph.D. in Computational Chemistry from Pondicherry University. Extensive international research experience in computational chemistry, quantum chemistry, and molecular dynamics simulations. Research interests include photonics, biophotonics, and theoretical chemistry.
